FAQ

Q: How do I verify my email?
A: After signing up, you’ll receive a verification link via email. Click that link to verify your account.
Q: I can’t log in. What should I do?
A: Make sure your email is verified. If you forgot your password, reset it through Password Recovery.
Q: How do I list a Rubik’s cube for sale?
A: In the competition tab, tap the “+” icon, fill out the form with cube details, set your price, and submit.
Q: How do I chat with a seller?
A: Tap on any listing, then select “Message Seller” to start a conversation.

Troubleshooting & Tips

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
  • Update to the latest version of WeCube for new features and bug fixes.
  • Clear app cache in your device settings if you experience performance issues.
